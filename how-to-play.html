How to Play FreeCell Game

Welcome to FreeCell Game, your new favorite place to play the classic card game. The goal is simple: move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, from Ace to King, sorted by suit.

Unlike other solitaire games, all cards are visible from the start, making FreeCell a game of skill rather than luck. Use the four "free cells" in the top-left corner as temporary storage to maneuver your cards.
